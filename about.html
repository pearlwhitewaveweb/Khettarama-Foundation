About Us

Welcome to Khettarama Foundation

Preserving Spirituality, History, and Wisdom

Rooted in Sri Lanka’s ancient heritage, the Khettarama Foundation is a center for spiritual enlightenment, historical preservation, and community service. We are committed to fostering cultural appreciation and promoting peace through our diverse programs and initiatives.

Through meditation retreats, cultural research, and local community support, our foundation seeks to bridge the past with the present and inspire a brighter future for all.

Our Leadership

Most Venerable Kovane Palitha Thero

The Khettarama Foundation is guided by the wisdom and dedication of our Honorable Director, Chief Incumbent, and Deputy Head of Pirivena, Most Venerable Kovane Palitha Thero. As a spiritual leader and All-Island Justice of the Peace, he has devoted his life to preserving Sri Lanka’s spiritual and cultural heritage.

With decades of service to the community, Most Venerable Kovane Palitha Thero continues to lead efforts in meditation, education, and cultural preservation, fostering a harmonious and enlightened society.
